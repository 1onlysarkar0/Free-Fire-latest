# 1OnlySarkar Free Fire Tournaments

## About
Join daily Free Fire tournaments on 1onlySarkar — Solo, Duo & Squad. Low entry fees, real cash prize pools, and instant UPI withdrawal. Register free and start competing today!

## Tournament Structure & Payouts
- **FREE Tournaments** — No entry fee, open to all registered players.
- **PAID Tournaments** — Requires wallet balance entry fee in INR.
- **Prize Payouts** — Credited directly to the UPI wallet after match verification, and withdrawable via bank/UPI.
